about me

I'm Mariele and I'm a web developer and graphic designer. I enjoy learning and making design concepts, and transforming it into interactive and responsive websites with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap (a CSS Framework), and JavaScript. And making the digital space safe and enjoyable for everyone.

To that end, I built this portfolio, and redesigned and coded the old website in which I initially developed for a startup company Vilein: Venrif. I also revamped Mensch's sticker label.

I continue to study more about web development, following online courses, building projects like Google-clone, Cat Checklist, Homestead, and Interactive-rating-component.

When not coding, I spend my time in the kitchen experimenting with new recipes, or taking a walk with my cat, Biscuit, or just hanging out with my boyfriend playing video games.
